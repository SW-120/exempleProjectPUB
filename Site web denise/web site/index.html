ALLO FO, THE FIRST SUCCESSFUL SINGLE The first one, Allo fo, invites us to dance together without caring disappointments of the daily life. Accompanied by talented producers (Kimfu, High P, Black Kent, 2B, Thomas Azier ), the artist shows herself such as she is in real life : a wife fascinated by Shyn, her husband with whom she writes and composes. One show-girl assumed lend of fire stadiums integer. An independent woman tells glimmers of hope and winning love. Denise, it is also a visual universe arisen from its collaboration with the Cameroonian designer Pierre-Christophe Gam. Both artists share love of Africa and will to represent the new generation. Far from stereotypes and moralizing speeches, they wish to address the African youth under quite its diversity. Shows the colors of it which decorate this project. Carried by a stalk of innocence and a infinite determination, Denise wants to see her dream coming true : travel in the world and in the people’s hearts.
